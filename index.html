Empowering Jamaica's Youth Through AI

"Out of Many, One People, One Future"

We're building the next generation of Jamaican tech leaders. Free AI education for Black youth across Jamaica, combining cutting-edge technology with our vibrant culture and limitless potential.

Our Mission

JAFUTURISTIC AI is dedicated to providing free, world-class artificial intelligence education to Black youth in Jamaica. We believe that every child deserves access to the skills that will shape tomorrow's world. Through hands-on learning, mentorship, and community support, we're creating opportunities for young Jamaicans to become the innovators, entrepreneurs, and leaders of the AI revolution.

Our Vision

We envision a future where Jamaica is recognized as a global hub for AI innovation and technological excellence. Our vision is to empower every Jamaican youth with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to compete on the world stage, while using technology to uplift their communities and preserve our rich cultural heritage. We see a Jamaica where technology and tradition work hand in hand to create sustainable prosperity for all.
