Our Mission

JAFUTURISTIC AI is dedicated to providing free, world-class artificial intelligence education to Black youth in Jamaica. We believe that every child deserves access to the skills that will shape tomorrow's world. Through hands-on learning, mentorship, and community support, we're creating opportunities for young Jamaicans to become the innovators, entrepreneurs, and leaders of the AI revolution.
